What's the point of this daily checklist anyway?
Revisit this page whenever you feel lost.

  1. You want to travel the world with a beautiful boy like Kevin.
  2. You want to be high on energy while doing that, so you need a healthy body.
  3. You want to be using your gifts and pursuing your passions to bring beauty into the creation,
    so you can fulfill your purpose as infinite being in this life form.
  4. You want to have a positive vibe about you, so you can move energy in positive ways
    to create positive and divinely beautiful things.
  5. Very importantly, you don't wanna be worrying about small existential things like money.
  6. You want to be lead intuitively by infinite being and be creative in ways this body was designed to do.
  7. You want to reach a stage where you help others identify their divine purpose rather than being gullible and getting discouraged in your purpose by their small-mindedness.
  8. You want to have steely self-confidence in your purpose, abilities and value in human society.
